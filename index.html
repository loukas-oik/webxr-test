VR Explorer

On Oculus Quest: tap the VR button (bottom-right).
Right trigger — hold to aim teleport arc, release to jump.
Left trigger — point at a cube to read its lore.

On desktop: WASD + mouse drag · click a cube to inspect.

⚠️ Must be served via HTTP, not opened as a file.
Run npx serve . in this folder, then open localhost:3000
